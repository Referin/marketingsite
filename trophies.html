Just the FAQs

Why do I have to register with Hearty (and Linkedin) to get my trophy?

We need you to create and account so that we can verify your identity and create an NFT wallet for your trophies.

Can I share this with other winners, such as my teammates who are part of this award?

Yes! They must register or log in at Hearty and select the appropriate trophy in our store. They will be asked to submit proof of their eligibility in the checkout process.

May I move this to another wallet?

You earned it, you own it, and you can move it to another wallet if you wish. Simply request a transfer within our app and we'll help you through the process.

Can I sell my Trophy NFT?

Yes. However the Hearty wallet is not built for purchase or exchange of Trophies among members, so you will have to move it to another wallet such as (Open Sea?)

Where can I request new Trophies?

You'll find a Request form on the Hearty Trophy Store. Note that we have a high demand and are prioritizing award requests from organizations and companies.

Who's behind this project?

Hearty was founded in 2020 by a team that has built successful companies together in the Web1 and Web2 waves. We’re building Hearty for Web3 and we’re on a mission to scale what we love doing most: Helping great people discover each other and accomplish great things together.

If you're ready to upgrade your network, we'd love to have you aboard.
